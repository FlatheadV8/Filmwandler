#
# MP@H-14 - HDV
#         - 1440x1080@30   60 Mbit/s
#         - 1280x720@30    60 Mbit/s
#
# MP@HL   - HD-DVB (HDTV)
#         - 1920x1080@30   80 Mbit/s
#         - 1280x720@60    80 Mbit/s
